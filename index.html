üåç Where Do Inbound Students Come From?

India and China continue to make up just over half of the international student population in Canada. While the number of Chinese students declined slightly after the 2020 pandemic, it remains substantial. Additionally, there has been significant growth in students from the Philippines and Nigeria, now the third and fourth largest sources of international students in Canada by 2023.
